AIR CDRE T OLADEGA

DSS psc fdc (+) BSc PGDE MEd MSc MBA PhD ACIPM MNIM FIMC FIPM MICSAN
  1. Home
  2. Leadership
  3. DOA

1. Air Cdre Taofiki Oladega was born on 23 Nov 1966 at No 11 Balogun Street Mushin, Lagos State. He is a native of Idimu at Alimosho Local Government Area of Lagos State. He attended Odu Abore PrimarySchool Mushin and Universal Primary Education School Festac Town Lagos. After his primary education, he attended both the Awori Anglican Comprehensive High School Ipaja and Awori Ajeromi Grammar School Agboju Amuwo Lagos. He is a member of 37 NDA Regular Course. He was enlisted into the NAF in 1985 and commissioned as a Plt Offr on 27 September 1990.


2. The Military Course attended by Air Cdre Oladega Taofiki include the Nigeria Defence Academy, 1989, Flying Training at 301 Flying School, Nigerian Air Force, Kaduna, 1990 and Initial Personnel and Administrative Management Officer’s course at 325 Ground Training Group, Nigerian Air Force, Kaduna, 1996. Others Courses include the Junior Division Course at the Armed Forces and Command and Staff College, Jaji in 2000,Senior Division Course at the Armed Forces and Command and Staff College, Jaji in 2002, Military Observer’s Course at the Royal Netherlands Army School of Peace Keeping Holland in 2001 and the National Defence College in 2013.


3. The appointments held by Air Cdre Oladega since commissioned includes Admin Officer, Academic Wing, 301 Flying Training School, Kaduna, Admin Officer, Headquarters Personnel Management Group, Ikeja and Staff Officer 2 Recruitment at the Directorate of Recruitment Resettlement and Reserve, Headquarters Nigerian Air Force. He also held the following appointment including Staff Officer 2 Personnel at Directorate of Personnel Management, Headquarters Nigerian Air Force, Chief Instructor and Acting Commanding Officer School of Administration, 325 Ground Training Group, Kaduna and Commanding Officer Admin Services Wing 335 Base Services Group, Nigeria Air Force, Kaduna. Others also includes Staff Officer 1 Admin HQ Logistic Command, Staff Officer 1 Admin (Directorate of Administration) Headquarters Nigerian Air Force, Command Admin Officer, Headquarters Logistics Command, 2012 and Commanding Officer Separation Benefit Wing at the then 104 Personnel Management Group, Lagos. He was also the Command Admin Officer, Headquarters Logistics Command, Lagos 2014, Directing Staff, National Defence College, Abuja, 2014, Director of Information, Communication, Technology National Defence College, 2015 and Director of Administration, Headquarters Nigerian Air force, 2015, Deputy Director Physical Training Defence Headquarters, 2016, Command Admin Officer, Training Command Nigerian Air Force, Base Kaduna, 2016-2017 and Deputy Director Innovation and Concept Development Defence Headquarters (2017 – 2019). Presently, Air Cdr T Oladega is the Director of Administration, Nigerian Armed Forces Resettlement Centre, Oshodi February 2019 till date.


4. The Military Awards and Medals possessed by Air Cdre Oladega includes, Pass Junior Staff Course (pjsc), pass senior course (psc), Forces Services Staff (FSS), Meritorious Services Star (MSS), Fellow of National Defence College (fdc), Distinguish Fellow of National Defence College (fdc+) and Distinguish Services Star (DSS).


5. The Educational Qualifcations/Institutions attended by Air Cdre Oladega also includes the West African School Certificate, and General Certificate of Education 1985 and 1990, Bachelor of Science (BSc Hons) in Chemistry (Second Class Upper Division) at the Nigerian Defence Academy, 1989. Other academic qualifications include Post Graduate Diploma in Education (PGDE) at the University of Calabar, 2000, Master in Business Administration (MBA) at the National Open University of Nigeria Abuja, 2015 and Master of Science in Strategic Studies (MSc) at the University of Ibadan, 2014. He also had a Masters in Education (MEd) in Educational Administration and Planning at the University of Calabar, 2002 and Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Educational Administration and Planning at the Lagos State University Ojo, 2019.


6. Air Cdre Oladega belongs to the following Professional Membership the Associate Member, Chartered Institute of Personnel Management of Nigeria (ACIPMN), Fellow Institute of Management Consultants (FIMC), Member Nigerian Institute of Management (MNIM), 2014, Member Institute of Chartered Secretaries and Administrators of Nigeria, (ICSAN) and Fellow Institute of Public Management of Nigeria (FIPM), 2012.


7. Air Cdre Oladega is happily married with 3 Children. His military and educational decorations are DSS psc fdc (+) BSc PGDE MEd MSc MBA PhD ACIPM MNIM FIMC FIPM MICSAN.
