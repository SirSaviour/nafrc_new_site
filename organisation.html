Organisation

  1. Home
  2. Organisation

The structure of Nigerian Armed Forces Resettlement Centre is composed of the Commandant (Comdt), Deputy Commandant (Dy Comdt), 5 Directors and other components which help in the preparation of trainees for re-integration into civil life upon retirement from active service. The Comdt is the Chief Training Officer and Administrator of NAFRC. He is assisted by the Dy Comdt who is also the Director of Studies.
