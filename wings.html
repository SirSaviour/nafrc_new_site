NAFRC Wings

  2. Wings

NAFRC Training Objectives are geared towards attaining the assigned goals in ensuring that retiring/discharging military personnel are trained and that at the end of the training, every trainee should be able to settle down, live in peace and contribute to the socio-economic development of any legitimate community he chooses or in which he finds himself after retirement/discharge.

Trainees receive practical and theoretical training in the centre in the course of six months before being awarded a certificate and carrying out industrial training in a reputable related establishment.

The Centre training courses are grouped in wings and each wing has a group of Wings under it. These Wings help in the practical programmes of each courses.

1. AGRO-ALLIED WING

This wing caters for trainees who are interested in the agricultural field of entrepreneurship. It is divided into two parts - Crop farming and Animal husbandry. Wings under this wing are Poultry, Fishery, Rabbitry, Ruminant (cow, goat, pig), Snailery, Cash and Food Crop Farming. Horticulture (study of plants, fruits and vegetables), floriculture and Ranching are Wings in this wing.

2. AUTOMOBILE WING

In this wing, trainees are taught the fundamental basics of Automobile Engineering, repairs and maintenance. These repairs and maintenance include battery charging, vulcanizing, car washing, glass and screen repairs. Paint making is also a workshop in this wing.

3. BODY CARE WING

In this wing, trainees learn how to make soap and cosmetics products. These products range from detergents, bar soap, liquid soap for bathing and washing (clothes, dishes etc.) body creams, hand sanitizers, air freshers with varieties of fragrances. A unisex salon is also a workshop in this wing, where trainees would learn how to make and cut hair, manicure and pedicure.

4. CREATIVE ARTS AND MUSIC WING

The creative aspect of life is being explored on this wing. Trainees get to learn about Creative/Fine art, Ceramics making, Photography, and Foundry. Music is also learned, including how to read and compose music, singing andlearning how to play any musical instrument.

5. EQUIPMENT INSTALLATION WING

In this wing, the Wings available are to teach trainees how to install, repair and maintain electrical/electronic appliances. Appliances such as televisions, radios, refrigerators, and air conditioners.

6. FABRICATION WING


7. FASHION FACILITY/DESIGNING WING

Fashion and designing provided in this wing ranges from tailoring (clothes), leatherproducts, Weaving (making of clothes) and laundry.

8. INFO-TECH DEPARTMENT

Trainees under this department are trained on how to operate, maintain, and repair computers. Courses like Networking, Hardware Engineering, Desktop Publishing, Graphics Design, Website and Software Development are also taught in the department.

9. MANAGEMENT WING

This wing caters to all participants (Trainees) and also a selected few who choose to learn under this wing.
Guidance and Counselling, a department in this wing help trainees in choosing which wing or workshop they can fit into perfectly.
General management department of this wing teaches trainees how to establish and manage a small-scale business. Accounting, Marketing, Personnel Management, Business Law are also topics taught in the department. Entrepreneur and General Studies, Security and Safety Practice, Waste Management are Wings in this wing.

10. NUTRITION AND DIETETICS WING

In this wing, trainees are taught the basic functions and healthy benefits of food. How to cook and what to eat to achieve a healthy lifestyle is the main objective of this wing.

11. PROPERTY DEVELOPMENT/ MAINTENANCE WING

Trainees in this wing are taught the basic fundamentals of building and construction of houses, roads and bridges. They are taught how to lay blocks and bricks, plumbing and pipefitting, painting and spraying. Woodworks such as Carpentry & Joinery, Furniture Design and Construction are taught. Machines to enhance the construction and design of wood work are also integrated in the courses of this wing.
