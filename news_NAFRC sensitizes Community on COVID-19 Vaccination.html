NAFRC Sensitizes Community on COVID-19 Vaccination.

March 18, 2021

The Nigerian Armed Forces Resettlement Centre (NAFRC) recently held a sensitization lecture in the COVID-19 vaccination for members of the centre’s community.

During the lecture, Air Vice Marshal Mohammed Adamu Idris, the centre’s Commandant, emphasized on the importance of getting vaccinated and the need to keep all COVID-19 safety precautions.

Wing Commander Samuel Oguntuase, the Commanding Officer, Medicals, stressed the reason why the vaccine was created as well as its importance; adding that the vaccine was not compulsory but necessary as it has been scientifically proven to help in the fight against the virus.

Mr. Best Ijori, one of the resource persons of the event gave a brief history of the coronavirus, where it originated, how the virus spreads and the number of recorded cases worldwide. He further explained the different species of the vaccine, the dosage and the range of people eligible to take the vaccine; informing his audience to take the vaccination process serious.

The event which was held at the Buhari Hall NAFRC had in attendance the Commandant, the Deputy Commandant, the directors, principal officers, Ministry of Defence /civilian staff, Youth Corp Members and others.
