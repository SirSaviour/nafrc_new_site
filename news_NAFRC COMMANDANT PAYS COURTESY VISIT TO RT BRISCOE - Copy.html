NAFRC COMMANDANT PAYS COURTESY VISIT TO RT BRISCOE.

July 31, 2020

Following the appointment and assumption in office as the 37th Commandant of the Nigerian Armed Forces Resettlement Centre, AVM M.A Idris and the management of NAFRC paid courtesy visit to RT Briscoe.

The Commandant who was pleased with the reception appreciated the managing director and staff for hosting then as he urged them to build on the existing relationship to make it stronger. He further solicited for their continued support adding that the Centre is willing to come to their aid at any point in time. The managing director, Mr Bukola Olajide congratulated the Commandant while he expressed gratitude for hosting him alongside his management team.

The MD who stated that RT Briscoe has maintained a stronger relationship with NAFRC since 2004, commended the previous commandants adding that, they have come to the aid of the company during very difficult times in the past.
