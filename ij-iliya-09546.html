CDRE IJ ILIYA

  1. Home
  2. Leadership
  3. DOL

1.     Commodore Ignatius James Iliya is from Karim-Lamido Local Government Area of Taraba State. He was born on 27 September 1965 in Numan, Numan Local Government Area of Adamawa State to Mr Iliya James and Mrs Maria Iliya. He started his early education at the Army Children Primary School, Kawo-Kaduna between 1972 and 1974, and completed it at the Army Children Primary School, Tunga-Minna in 1979. Thereafter, he went to the Nigerian Military School, Zaria for his secondary education from 1979 to 1984 before proceeding to the Nigerian Defence Academy from 1984 to 1988, where he obtained a Single Honours Bachelors of Science Degree in Chemistry. He joined the Nigerian Navy as a Midshipman in September 1988 and was commissioned Sub-Lieutenant with effect from September 1989. He steadily progressed in his career, and was promoted to the rank of Commodore in 2012.


2.     He started his naval career as a Watch Keeping Officer on board Nigerian Navy Ship OFIOM from January 1990 to April 1991 and was later appointed to Nigerian Navy Ship AMBE also as Watch Keeping Officer from April 1991 to November 1991. He left for the Sub-Lieutenant Technical Course onboard Nigerian Navy Ship QUORRA from November 1991 to December 1992, after which he was appointed as Watch Keeping Officer/Watch Keeping Duties on board Nigerian Navy Ship LANA from January 1993 to May 1994. Thereafter he was appointed Duties with Commanding Officer Nigerian Navy Ship OKEMINI (now Nigerian Navy Ship PATHFINDER) from May 1994 to August 1997 before being appointed Duties with Officer in Charge at the Forward Operations Base IBAKA from August 1997 to July 1998. He proceeded to the Armed Forces Command and Staff College, Jaji for his Junior Staff Course from July to December 1998. After the course, he was appointed as Naval Assistant to the Chief Naval Officer ECOMOG Naval Task Force, Operations SANDSTORM Sierra Leone from January to October 1999. He left for his Officersâ€™ Long Specialization Course at the Nigerian Navy Ship QUORRA from October 1999 to December 2000.


3.     On completion of the specialization course, he was appointed the Executive Officer Nigerian Navy Ship BRASS from February 2001 to August 2002 and then as an Instructor at the Above Water Warfare School, Nigerian Navy Ship QUORRA from August 2002 to July 2003. He was then nominated for the Senior Staff Course at the Armed Forces Command Staff Course from August 2003 to July 2004. After the course, he was appointed as Staff Officer Grade II, Above Water Warfare at the Sea Training Command from July 2004 to January 2005 and subsequently to Nigerian Navy Ship WEY as Base Administrative Officer from January to June 2004. He was thereafter nominated as a United Nations Military Observer with MONUC (now MONUSCO) in the Democratic Republic of Congo from June 2005 to Jun 06. On completion of his tour of duty with MONUC, he was appointed Staff Officer Grade I, Above Water Warfare Tactics at the Training and Operations Branch of Naval Headquarters from August 2006 to January 2008. After promotion to the rank of Navy Captain, he was appointed as Base Commander at the Nigerian Navy Finance and Logistics School, Owerrinta from January to September 2008.


4.     From the Nigerian Navy Finance and Logistics School, he proceeded to Liberia as Senior United Nations Military Observer with the United Nations Mission in Liberia (UNMIL) from September 2008 to March 2010. After the assignment, he was appointed Deputy Director Combat Policy at the Training and Operations Branch of Naval Headquarters from April to November 2010, and then as Director of Logistics at the Nigerian Naval College ONNE from November 2010 to September 2011 when he proceeded to the prestigious National Defence College Nigeria in Abuja from September 2011 to August 2012. After the course, he went to the University of Ibadan and obtained Masterâ€™s Degree in Strategic Studies in May 2013. He has held several appointments after his stint at University of Ibadan amongst which include Deputy Director Special Operations Naval Headquarters, Commander Nigerian Navy Ship WEY, Deputy Superintendent Naval Ordnance Depot and Director of Administration at Naval Headquarters. Others are Commandant Nigerian NAVY School of Armament Technology KACHIA, Deputy Superintendent Naval Doctrine and Assessment Centre and currently Deputy Director Target Development at the Defence Intelligence Agency (DIA). Cdre Iliya had the privilege of attending the Senior Executive Entrepreneurship and Management Course in September 2020 at the Nigerian Armed Forces Resettlement Centre Oshodi, facilitated by Empretec Nigeria Foundation, under the auspices of United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD). The senior officer is currently serving at the Nigerian Armed Forces Resettlement Centre (NAFRC), Oshodi as the Director of Logistics with effect from 17 March 2021.


5.     Commodore Iliya is a Certified Protection Officer (CPO) earned under the auspices of International Foundation for Protection Officers (IFPO) in 2016, as well as a Certified Protection Professional (CPP) earned under the auspices of American Society for Industrial Security (ASIS) International in 2019. He is member of several professional bodies including Chemical Society of Nigeria, Institute of Chattered Chemist of Nigeria and United States Naval Institute. Others are Association of Industrial Security and Safety Operators of Nigeria, International Foundation for Protection Officers and American Society for Industrial Security International. During his military career, he has been awarded the Forces Service Star, Meritorious Service Star, Distinguished Service Star and Grand Service Star. For his participation in Peace Support Operations, he earned the ECOMOG, MONUC and UNMIL medals. His hobbies include working-out, watching documentaries/sports, listening to jazz/rhythm and blues music, driving and swimming. He is married to Mrs Rebecca Laraba Iliya and the union is blessed with 5 boys.
