BRIG GEN AY BWALA   DSS psc fdc CM GOM MNIM BSc

  1. Home
  2. Leadership
  3. DOT

1. Brig Gen Adamu Yakubu Bwala was born on 22 Jun 64 to the family of late Wakil Adamu Dika Bwala and late Aisha Pindar of Gwaski Village, Hawul local Government Area, Borno State. He had his primary, secondary and post-secondary education in Gwaski, Waka-Biu and Maiduguri respectively between 1971 and 1987. He graduated from University of Maiduguri with BSc (Hons) Geology in 1987 and served his National Youth Service Corp with Shell Petroleum Development Company of Nigeria, Warri in 1987/1988 service year. He worked briefly with Borno State Water Board as geologist before joining the Nigerian Army on 5 Jan 90 as a member of 29 Short Service Course. He was commissioned on 22 Sep 90 and posted to Nigerian Army Corps of Artillery.


2. He has served in various units, Bdes and AHQ some of which are, 322 AR Obinze, 342 AR ECOMOG, HQ 33 Bde, HQ 30 Arty Bde ECOMOG, HQ NIGCON ECOMOG, Sierra Bty 155 Op Harmony, Depot NA, AHQ DOAA, Arty Sp AHQ Ops NE,35 Bde Gar, NASA, 34 Bde Gar and 302 AR {GS}. During the course of his career, he was trained as a Gnr officer at the Nigerian Army School of Arty both in Kontagora and Kachia.


3. Brig Gen Bwala has attended several courses amongst which are; YOC Arty, YOC Armour, Arty Svy Offrs Course, Regtl Offrs Gnry Course, Junior Staff Course, Bty Comd Course, Senior Staff Course, System Approach to Trg, Military Observer Course in Nairobi, Regtl Commanding Offrs Course and National Defence College Course 25 (NDC 25). He has held several appointment which include, Tp Comd, Adjt, MA to NIGCON Comd, Op Liberty and Sandstorm, Bty Comd, G4 DEPOT NA, AD Admin AHQ DOAA, CO Arty Sp Ops AHQ NE, OP BOYONA/ZAMAN LAFIYA, Member Training Team (NASA), Gar Comd 35 Bde, and Gar Comd 34 Bde.


4. His operational experiences include ECOMOG Ops in Liberia, Op Sandstorm in Sierra Leone, Op Harmony iv, Op FLUSH OUT 2, Op RESTORE Order.
