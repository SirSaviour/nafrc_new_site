Our Vision

To be a world class Training Institution capable of repositioning ex service men and women to cope with challenges of service in their post service life.

Mission Statement

To consistently provide quality training, geared towards preparing Nigerian Armed Forces personnel to face the challenges of re-integrating into civil life.

Objectives

“Our training objectives are geared towards attaining the assigned goals in ensuring that retiring/discharging military personnel are trained such that at the end of the training it is expected that every trainee should be able to settle down, live in peace, and contribute to the socio economic development of any legitimate community he chooses or in which he finds himself after retirement discharge."

Our Departments

AGRIC

Caters for trainees who are interested in the agricultural field of entrepreneurship. It is divided into two parts - Crop farming and Animal husbandry.

AUTO MECHANIC

This Wing encompasses several sub-units. It comprised the Automobile Mechanics, Auto Glass Windscreen Repair, Battery Charging and Car-wash workshops.

BODY CARE

Trainees are tutored on how to make soap and cosmetics products ranging from detergents, bar soap, liquid soap, body creams, hand sanitizers, etc.

BUILDING/ CIVIL WORKS

Trainees in this wing are taught the basic fundamentals of building and construction of houses, roads and bridges.

ELECTRICAL ELECTRONICS

In this wing, the Wings available are to teach trainees how to install, repair and maintain electrical/electronic appliances.

FABRICATION & WELDING

The mandate of the Fabrication and wielding wing is to impact technical skill on the about to disengaged service personnel to enable them to be self employed and employers of labour after retirement.

FASHION FACILITIES

Fashion and designing provided in this wing ranges from tailoring (clothes), leather products, Weaving (making of clothes) and laundry

FINE ARTS & PRINTING

This wing encompasses three notable workshops under it, these workshops are the Printing Press, Fine Arts & Ceramics and Photo Studio (Photography).

FOOD & BREVERAGES

Trainees are taught the basic functions and healthy benefits of food. How to cook and what to eat to achieve a healthy lifestyle.

INFO-TECH

Trainees under this department are trained on how to operate, maintain, and repair computers.

MANAGEMENT

This department is saddled with the responsibility of equipping trainees with basic rudiment of Management Skills, Security and Safety Management, Waste Management, and Entrepreneurship.

MUSIC

The creative aspect of life is being explored in this wing. Trainees learn reading and writing music and they also learn to play musical instruments of their choice. They are also instructed in the business of music.

WOOD WORKS

At Woodwork Wing, the Trainees will be taught various aspects of Capentry, Furniture and Machine works.
